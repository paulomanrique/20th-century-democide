This Ring of Tears is a photomontage--a megatomb--memorializing the 174,000,000 murdered by governments 1900-1999. It merges photos of their corpses, bones, and skulls. For documentation on the number murdered and the what, when, where, and who, see this web site. For the photos that were used in the megatomb, go to (or return to) Photographs of Democide: .

A second megatomb, [Rings of Tears: The Murdering](RM2.RINGS.OF.TEARS2.HTM), shows the process of murder by governments. The photos for this megatomb are at Photographs of Democide: [Room 2](RM2.PHOTOS.ROOM2.HTM). The final megatomb, [Rings of Tears: Faces of Death](RM3.RINGS.OF.TEARS3.HTM), displays the faces of those about to die, most knowing it. The photos for this megatomb are at Photographs of Democide: [Room 3](RM3.PHOTOS.ROOM3.HTM).
