This page is the canonical index for the twentieth-century democide material on the `powerkills` site. It combines a top-level estimate summary, direct links into multiple Rummel books and articles, and later revisions that moved key totals upward.

## Preservation note

The repository treats this page as the founding inventory for the first recovery track. All direct links on the page are candidates for local preservation, classification, and publication.

## Notable revisions referenced on the page

- Mao's famine estimate is revised upward to `38,000,000`.
- PRC democide total is revised from `38,702,000` to `76,702,000`.
- Colonial democide is revised upward by `50,000,000`.
- The page states a revised world total of `262,000,000`.

## Corpus role

The `20TH.HTM` page is not just an article. It is the spine of the initial repository inventory because it links to:

- book notes for *Death by Government*, *Lethal Politics*, *China's Bloody Century*, *Democide*, and *Statistics of Democide*;
- summary tables and figures;
- articles on definitions, communism, the Holocaust, and postwar democide;
- linked subchapters and supporting visual material.
