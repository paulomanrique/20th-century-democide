# CONFLICT  
AND VIOLENCE

  
  

> Most of my early precomputer theoretical and empirical work was on conflict, violence and war, the most important of which I will be adding here as I am able to scan them into the computer. The material included here deals generally with conflict and peace; that specifically relating to the democratic peace is included on that . The conclusion from all this research is that the more freedom people have, the less their collective violence and war.
